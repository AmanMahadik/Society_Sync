Core Operating Systems

Five connected modules that automate security gates, legal ledgers, democratic polling, and asset maintenance.

Smart Visitor Parking

Residents book designated visitor parking slots (V1-V10) instantly. Gate entry requests are logged, and availability updates in real-time upon security desk approvals.

Festival Ledger Sheets

Complete transparent accounting for community festivals. Committee members record contributions and vendor bills, upload receipts using device image pickers, and export high-fidelity PDF report ledgers.

Maintenance Ledgers

Monthly society maintenance is calculated and recorded. Supports delinquent leaderboards, automatic late-fee calculations (2%), and push notification payment reminders.

Emergency SOS Crisis Alarms

Trigger instant high-priority alarms for water, electricity, or security crises. Guards are alerted in real-time, and resolving an SOS pushes feedback notifications to all administrators.

Council Chat & Polling

Engage in categorized debates (Infrastructure, Budget, Events). Create democratic voting polls with expiration thresholds and pinned alerts to make decisions together.

Help Desk

Council Help Center

Step-by-step guides to help residents, owners, and council administrators navigate their digital accounts.

Resident Registration

Complete your flat details (Wing, Flat Number, Phone) and upload your profile picture. All registrations enter a Pending Review queue and must be approved by the Society Administrator before accessing portal modules.

Paying Dues

Go to the Maintenance Dues tab to view outstanding payments. Overdue invoices automatically calculate a 2% interest penalty after the due date. Click Pay Dues Online to proceed through the integrated secure gateway.

Voting in Council

Admins and owners can publish voting polls in any chat thread. All registered society members can cast one vote per poll. Results are updated in real-time, and polls freeze automatically once their expiration date passes.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries regarding parking rules, SOS protocols, and financial audits.

Who can approve visitor parking requests?
Only administrators (Society Secretary/Management Committee) have access to approve or reject visitor parking requests. When a resident submits a parking request, a push notification is sent to the administrators who review the booking and assign the slot.
What happens when I trigger an SOS alert?
Triggering an SOS sends an emergency alert notification to all security guards and society administrators. The alert flashes at the gate guard terminal. Once a guard acknowledges and resolves the issue, they log feedback that notifies the management committee that the crisis has been resolved.
How do I export a festival financial report?
Go to the Finances screen, select the active festival event, and click the PDF icon. The application compiles all recorded contributions and expense invoices, formats them into a premium multi-page ledger statement matching our design specifications, and opens the sharing/download sheet on your device.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes. All data and transaction ledgers are stored in a secured Supabase PostgreSQL database. Access is controlled using Row Level Security (RLS) policies, ensuring only authenticated and approved residents can read ledgers, and only authorized committee members can modify transaction registers.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

SocietySync is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of residents, owners, tenants, and security staff of housing societies using our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly provided by you during registration and profile completion, including: your full name, email address, phone number, wing, flat number, vehicle license number, and profile image. If you upload transaction proofs, we store the receipt image files securely.

2. Use of Information

We use your information solely to provide core society features, including: gating portal access for approved residents, recording flat-wise maintenance dues, booking visitor parking slots, sending push notification reminders, and managing emergency SOS communications.

3. Data Security

All communications, chat messages, ledger entries, and personal records are stored in databases protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies. Your data is never shared with, sold to, or distributed to third-party marketing companies.
